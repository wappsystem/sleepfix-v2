Welcome to SleepFix

Take part in an exciting online research study using a new smartphone application that aims to help improve your sleep and daytime functioning.

SleepFix is a smartphone application designed to treat insomnia through sleep consolidation therapy. Sleep consolidation therapy works to improve the balance between actual sleep time and the total amount of time spent in bed.

The study is for individuals with sleep problems who are aged between 18 and 65 and have access to a smartphone.

To determine if you are eligible to participate, you will need to complete a series of questionnaires which take less than 2 minutes. If you are eligible you will be invited to provide consent and take part in the study. The study will involve using the SleepFix application every day for three weeks. You can use the application for a further three weeks if you wish. You will also be asked to complete a series questionnaires (which take up to 5 minutes to complete) at the following timepoints; at start of the study, weekly (for 3 weeks), after 6 weeks and 12 weeks.

In addition, all participants will be randomly assigned to using SleepFix with a Fitbit which will track your sleep or SleepFix by itself. We will send you a Fitbit at no cost for you to use if you are in that group. If you already have a Fitbit that tracks your sleep you can use it during the study.

Your participation in this study is voluntary.

Please note that SleepFix is not optimised for tablet devices
